About the Application

A Payment book application designed to maintain customers, payments, and their purchases of an electronics store. A retailer will be an admin of the application and each customer of the retailer’s shop will be the user. Customers can create their account in the application with their details and they will be able to see their purchase history; and pending payments. Admin will maintain data about purchases made by the customers & can see payment details and pending payments of the customers and will send alerts to the customers via SMS if there is any due for payment or payment is pending for a long time.
